About Me

Hey there, I'm Nathan. Thanks for visiting my portfolio! This page is meant to tell you about my professional skills and qualifications, while giving a solid sample of my work.

I'm somewhat a jack of all trades but mostly, I am a skilled sales and marketing professional, with over 8 years of experience selling and marketing cars, heavy equipment parts/skid steer doors as well as constructing websites, managing digital campaigns, and building strong customer relationships.

Most recently, I’ve grown SkidSteerCabs.com through a strong sales process, SEO, email marketing, Google Ads, and Facebook campaigns. Before that, I managed digital marketing and vehicle sales at a Nissan dealership, and earlier I cut my teeth in a pawn shop, where I learned to think on my feet, assess item values, and connect with people from all walks of life.

Outside of sales and marketing, I'm passionate about researching new topics and ideas, often using OSINT techniques, learning about cybersecurity, and gaming. I enjoy learning to code, experimenting with Linux, and working on projects that mix creativity, and problem solving. When I'm not at my desk, you'll hanging out with my family or spending time outside.

My experience gives me a unique combination of excellent customer service skills, inbound & outbound sales, management, and marketing experience that I enjoy leveraging to create happy customers, and successful businesses.
