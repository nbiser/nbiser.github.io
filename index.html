About Me

Hi, I'm Nathan. I'm a skilled sales and marketing professional with over 8 years experience selling everything from cars to skid steer doors, managing websites, constructing digital campaigns, and building strong customer relationships.

Most recently, I’ve grown SkidSteerCabs.com through SEO, email marketing, Google Ads, Facebook campaigns, and a strong inbound sales process. Before that I managed digital marketing and vehicle sales at a Nissan dealership, and earlier I cut my teeth in a pawn shop, where I learned to think on my feet, access item values, and connect with people from all walks of life.

Outside of sales and marking I'm passionate about researching new topics and ideas, often using OSINT techniques, learning about cybersecurity, and gaming. I enjoy learning to code, experimenting with Linux, and working on projects that mix creativity and problem solving. When I'm not at my desk, you'll find me gaming, spending time outside, or hanging out with my family.

My experience gives me a unique combination of excelent customer service, inbound/outbound sales, management, and marketing skills that I love to leverage to create happy customers and succesful businesses.
